The Unbreakable Mindset.

You are a single Data Packet traveling through the vast network of the Google OAuth 2.0 flow. You’ve just left the Website and arrived at the Auth Server.

Security guards (Firewalls) are checking your headers. You have a Client_ID, but you’re missing your Secret. If you don't find it soon, the session will time out and the User will be stuck on a loading screen forever.
